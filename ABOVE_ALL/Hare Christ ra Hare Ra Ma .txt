In the Sanskrit sacred alphabet and tantric philosophy, "Ha" represents the ultimate source of life and cosmic power.

* The Flow of Wealth and Joy: In Sanskrit, the root Ri means to move or flow. It represents Shakti—the feminine cosmic energy, wealth, and abundance that flows into the individual soul.
