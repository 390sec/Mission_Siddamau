## 1. "Ra Ma" (Rama / Ram)
In Sanskrit and Hindi, Rama (राम) is one of the most sacred words. It is not just a name; it is made of two root syllables:

* Ra (रा): Means "Light", "Splendour", or the divine energy that illuminates the universe.
* Ma (म): Means "Myself", "Mine", or the individual soul.
* Together: "Rama" translates to "the light within me" or "the source of all joy".
