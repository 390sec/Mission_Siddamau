Until All Christ Words Fulfilled

Jesus taught in **Matthew 5:18** that **not an iota, not a dot** will pass from the Law until all is accomplished. He affirmed that **my words shall not pass away** (Matthew 24:35), emphasizing the eternal reliability of his message.

This teaching indicates that **heaven and earth may pass away**, but his words remain. The phrase "until all is accomplished" refers to the **fulfillment of all Scripture**, including the Law and the Prophets, which was completed through Christ's work.

*   **Key Verse**: "For truly, I say to you, until heaven and earth pass away, not an iota, not a dot, will pass from the Law until all is accomplished." (Matthew 5:18)
*   **Eternal Nature**: "Heaven and earth will pass away, but my words will not pass away." (Matthew 24:35)
*   **Fulfillment**: Jesus stated he came not to abolish the Law but to **fulfill** it, completing its prophetic and ceremonial requirements.
