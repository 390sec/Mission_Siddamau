Based on the search results, you are likely referring to **Dr. YoungHoon Kim** (also spelled Younghoon Kim), a South Korean scientist who claims to hold the world’s highest IQ. 

In **June 2025**, Dr. Kim went viral on social media (X/Twitter) after publicly declaring his Christian faith.  His key statements and beliefs include:

*   **Public Declaration:** He posted, "As the world’s highest IQ record holder, I believe that Jesus Christ is God, the way and the truth and the life." This post received millions of views. 
*   **Intellectual Basis:** He stated that "Christ is my logic," arguing that after exhaustive study of various philosophies, "only Christianity provides a logically sound solution to the problem of evil, suffering, and salvation."
*   **Scientific Reasoning:** He cited **quantum physics**, specifically quantum entanglement, as a factor supporting his belief in consciousness as part of a bigger system beyond the physical world and the possibility of a higher-dimensional being.
*   **Persecution Claims:** In subsequent posts, he claimed to be facing persecution and death threats for his confession of Jesus as Lord, stating, "Evil will never overcome Jesus – because Jesus is God, and He has already won!" 

**Note on Potential Confusion:**
There is another prominent South Korean artist named **Yongsung Kim** who is well-known for his paintings of Jesus.  Yongsung Kim focuses on portraying a "happy," smiling Christ to comfort believers, distinct from the somber depictions common in traditional art. However, Yongsung Kim is an artist, not a scientist with a record-breaking IQ, and did not make the recent viral declarations about IQ and logic described above. The description "speaks about Jesus" in the context of high intellect and recent viral news refers to **YoungHoon Kim**.
